The Relationship

A $5 \times 5$ convolution can be factored into a sequence of $1 \times 5$ and $5 \times 1$ operations:

  • PHASE 1 The $1 \times 5$ filter slides horizontally. It maps 5 spatial pixels into **1 intermediate value** (yellow).
  • PHASE 2 The $5 \times 1$ filter slides vertically over the intermediate values to produce the **final output** (green).

Output Size Logic

General Formula

$$O = \frac{I - F + 2P}{S} + 1$$

With $N=9, F=5, S=1, P=0$

$$O = (9 - 5) + 1 = 5$$

Final Map: $5 \times 5$
