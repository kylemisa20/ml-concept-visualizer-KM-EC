RoPE Visualizer

Unlike the original Transformer which adds positions, RoPE rotates the vectors based on their position. The magic is in the Dot Product: rotating two vectors by the same amount doesn't change the angle between them!

2D Rotation Formulation
\( \text{RoPE}(x, m) = \begin{pmatrix} \cos(m\theta) & -\sin(m\theta) \\ \sin(m\theta) & \cos(m\theta) \end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix} x_0 \\ x_1 \end{pmatrix} \)
Dot Product Property

The rotation matrices cancel out, leaving only the relative distance \((n-m)\):

\( \langle \text{RoPE}(Q, m), \text{RoPE}(K, n) \rangle = Q^T R_{n-m} K \)
